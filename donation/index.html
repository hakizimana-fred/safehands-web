Donation

Make a Donation to the Little Angels Orphan + At-risk Children Charity

Donate

Wanting to help and start helping! We cannot do without your support and that is why many hands make work lighter! We welcome donations from corporations, churches, foundations, rock stars, individuals, Volunteers and soon.

Little Angels Orphan + At-risk Children Charity is committed to promoting the well-being of orphans + at-risk children in Mbarara, Kabale, Kasese, Rukiga and in Rubanda Districts by providing food, shelter, medical help, academic opportunity, life training skills, mentoring, career placement, and health programs.

 

There are several ways in which you can support the work of Little Angels Ministry Uganda and please contact us if you can lend a hand in assisting the less privileged through one of the following:

  • Giving a cash donation for specific or general support.
  • Make a donation for Children Welfare Project (CWP).
  • Giving a one time, monthly or yearly donation toward a specific issue close to your heart, i.e. Orphans’ Education, combating HIV/AIDS, Orphans living in abject poverty, rural development and or providing information on possible sources of funding.
  • Fundraising for constructing Little Angels orphanage center.
  • Volunteer/Interns to work withLittle Angels Ministry Ugandafor a specific time.
  • Fundraising for Little Angels Ministry UgandaOVCs Education Project (MOEP)

 

Therefore we need your donations; act now in any way possible. No amount is too small not to be welcomed by Little Angels Ministry Uganda. Your contribution, however small, will have a great impact on lives of Girls and Boys both street kids of Mbarara, Kabale, Rubanda, Kasese and RukigaDistricts Uganda.

YOUR DONATION WILL BUY

  • $30 provides enough sanitary pads for a girl child in hard to reach communities in Mbarara, Kabale, Rubanda, Kasese and Rukiga Districts for a year so that she can keep in school.
  • $300 provides support for a community sensitization workshop on HIV/AIDS and poverty.
  • $600 provides a family with a cow for milk production and income generation
  • $300 helps an elder a year in the local communities
  • $40 provides a family goat for livestock development
  • $30 provides a helps a family to establish a kitchen garden to help improve their nutrition.
  • $30 provides a solar lantern so that a student can read after dark.
  • $60 provides breakfast and lunch for one student a year
  • $600 helps construct a single mother’s house

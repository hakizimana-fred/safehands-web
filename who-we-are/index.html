Safehands Children Foundation

Lifting Lives with Hands of Help.

Thank you for visiting our website of Safe hands children foundation Namayumba (SHCF) which is a Faith based community charity organization REG.NO.WCBO/19/2060 that was founded in October 2017. It is located in Uganda, Nakedde village in Wakiso district. The organization aims at providing spiritual, mental, social, and physical support to orphans, vulnerable children, widows, and needy children through welfare programs that are focused on a sustainable impact in the poor communities.
SHCF also fulfills Jesus's request of spreading the Good news and also helps the beneficiaries access Education, medical care, feeding, shelter through its sponsorship program. SHCF has outreaches under different emergencies and needs that help the people in the communities
SHCF also carries out mission work, guidance and counseling projects, culture uplift, and others.
Currently, the organization has 46 children who keep on icreasesing due to the narrowing levels of survivorship caused due poor and harsh conditions in the community.We also have a target of extending more lifting hands of help to more children in Uganda on the same cause of a quality better life and sustainable impact with a generous and imppact oriented person like you we can move an extra mile of change in peoples lives.

Through campaigns like the sanitary pads for girl child we managed to teach over 100 girls how to make themselves reuseable sanitary pads and also donated resuseable pads reducing on the number of reproductive inffections and low esteem among girls,blankets and mattress to children that reduced on the number of sleeping discomfort,illness that affected there growth and other campaigns.
This would not be have been possible without the incredibly kind and generous people around the world.THANK YOU and With greate opportunity comes responsiblity. We are determmined to help create more sustainable impact with YOU on board. So please stick with us, follow up our daily updates on our social platforms and help us change the lives of the children forever

CORE VALUES

At safe hands children foundation, we consider a successful drive that discloses every person to a meaningful service needs through a commitment to prayers, integrity, stewardship, servant leadership and lasting results to be done carefully. As to the Foundation's success our unifying mission, vision, goals, and measures are our unique values with the distinguishing attributes that characterize how we conduct ourselves in our drive for sustainable impact. These attributes are not a mere tag but deeply held convictions manifested by all who represent Safe hands children foundation.

PRAYER

We realize that praying helps us make our needs significant. A Prayer declares that we believe we need the power of God's presence to see our world changed for His glory and good. For our battle is not only physical, but the battle is a spiritual one. And that requires spiritual weapons which the major weapon is praying and thanksgiving.

RESPECTT AND DIGINITY

We consider the uniqueness and intrinsic value of every person the people we serve, the donors, our volunteers, staff, and the public. Since Enduring the success of our mission depends on the active involvement of people and partners whose lives are linked to the natural systems, we seek to help our beneficiaries. We respect the needs, values, and traditions of open space lands, local communities, and cultures connected to these special places, and we form relationships based on mutual benefit and trust. We value respectful, reasoned dialogue with one another when we disagree.

COMMUNITY

We operate with and exist in a community. We have a belief that it is our responsibility to impact them holistically. We will stand in solidarity with those who are marginalized by society.

ACCOUNTABILTY

We recognize that the true measure of our success if the children and the communities we serve change for the better as a result of our programs. We strive to produce measurable outcomes through program evaluations and we recognize that developing and maintaining sound financial management is an important element of our accountability..

INTEGRITY

We will meet the highest ethical standards in all of our Charity endeavors and, in doing so, we hold ourselves accountable to our mission. Through honesty being accountable to each other, mission, our donors, beneficiaries, partners, and the public..

STEWARDSHIP

: We can accomplish our mission through the generosity of others.As a foundation, we are always vigilant concerning a donor's intentions and restrictions on the use of their gifts and We promote responsible stewardship of the resources entrusted to us for the accomplishment of our work.

LOVE

Through love we show value to a child. Love requires us to live outside ourselves and give sacrificially. It is our motivation in everything we do.

SERVANT LEADERSHIP

we strive at getting real with the people we lead with the willingness to get hands dirty and shoulder the load with the people we serve. We focus on how we can connect in more meaningful ways.

CHILD-CENTERED

God has a plan and purpose for each child. We focus on children as individuals and encourage their dreams and aspirations. Our decisions and actions are guided by the best interest of the child.

PARTNERSHIP

We are committed to partnering with like-minded, charities, ministries, churches, Para- church organizations, and other agencies that subscribe to our mission and vision of our foundation.

EXECELLENCE

In keeping with our identity and foundation target uphold the highest ethical and professional standards in all working relationships, we are committed to a culture of excellence in all our service delivery and ministries. Challenging ourselves to the highest level of performance, tapping the best of the human spirit to create impact.

EMPOWERMENT

We strive to fix larger systemic problems and empower our beneficiaries to live a better life. We aim at tailoring it to the services that we provide.
